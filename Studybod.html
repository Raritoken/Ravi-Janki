Studybod



The Problem

"How can we stimulate secondary school students in an interactive manner to work together in order to take in the learning material more efficiently?"

Our Concepts

Above you can watch our presentation video of the three concepts we came up with (in Dutch).

The Solution

Studybod is an AI that supports secondary school students while working on assignments. The AI can link students with each other when it notices that a student has been working on an assignment for significantly longer than usual. Students can also seek/offer help themselves on the website where the Studybod AI matches the students based on skills.

What have I learned?

During this project I learned how to turn a very vague concept into a real product by brainstorming a lot and actively sketching/creating designs and/or features that would be related to the product. I've also found that it's very important to make things, even if I'm not quite sure how the product is going to look or work. I can then present this to my target group to receive feedback from them and to continue developing a suitable product for this target group.
