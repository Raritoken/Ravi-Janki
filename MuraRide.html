Project

MuraRide



The Problem

"How can we offer young people in villages a more efficient way to move to the city, so that they can go at their own times and experience less stress?"

The Solution

MuraRide is a solution for young people traveling more efficiently from villages to the city. MuraRide does this through the ANWB-app in which the user can find the nearest accessable vehicle on a so called "hub".

Through the ANWB-app the user can reserve the vehicle for a certain amount of time. Once at the vehicle, all the user needs to do to unlock the vehicle is scanning their phone using the ANWB-app. Once unlocked they can use the vehicle to travel to their destination and their rental time starts.
At the end of the rental time the user either returns the vehicle to the hub they took it from, or they can leave the vehicle at the nearest hub to their destination in order to render the vehicle accessible to other users.

Adding to that, owning an ANWB subscription would make rental of vehicles even more afforable because of the discount it'd include for the users.
