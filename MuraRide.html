Project

MuraRide



The Problem

"How can we offer young people in villages a more efficient way to move to the city, so that they can go at their own times and experience less stress?"

What have I learned?

During this project I learned how valuable good research really is, this has allowed my team and I to make better choices in order to arrive at concepts that are desirable for our target group and in which they have a lot of say.
